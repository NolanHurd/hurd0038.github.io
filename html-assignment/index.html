About Me

I am a student in the Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College. It's been three years since I've been in school but it feels like it's coming back incredibly naturally! I'm very much enjoying all of the courses that this program has to offer! Learning online from the comfort of my own home is an incredibly interesting and fun experience. It's my 1st time being educated this way, and so far it's going great!

I'm a very big fan of media, both in how it is created and how it is presented. The form of media I have the most interest in is video games, as I'm always in awe of the breathtaking visuals and intuitive gameplay that they have to offer. I look forward to new horizons and learning more as I partake in this program!
